Our Story

Founded in 2023, Vestra began with a simple question: "Why is it easier to buy crypto than it is to buy a rental property?"

Traditional real estate requires massive capital, endless paperwork, and property management headaches. We built a platform that handles the heavy lifting—legal compliance, LLC creation, and tenant management—so you can own shares of physical properties with the click of a button.
